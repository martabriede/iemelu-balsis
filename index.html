About us

The Latvian choir in Estonia Ziemeļu Balsis was founded in 2012. Today we unite singers from many countries and two cities — Tartu and Tallinn. Thanks to regular joint rehearsals, the choir has grown into a truly harmonious and united collective. We’re the first choir to sing in all three Baltic Song Festivals: Latvian (2013, 2018, 2023), Lithuanian (2024), and Estonian (2025).

Conductors

Matīss Briedis — member since 2013, conductor since 2017; studies at the Estonian Academy of Music and Theatre (MA in conducting).

Rihards Zariņš — studied at Riga Cathedral Choir School & Jāzeps Vītols Academy; has led choirs in Latvia, Estonia and Finland.
