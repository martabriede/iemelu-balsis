About the choir

Ziemeļu Balsis is a Latvian mixed choir active in Estonia with rehearsals in both Tartu and Tallinn. We gather weekly to celebrate music and Latvian choral traditions, led by our conductors Matīss Briedis and Rihards Zariņš.

  • Conductors: Matīss Briedis and Rihards Zariņš
  • Repertoire: Latvian choir music, world classics, contemporary works
  • Auditions: Light audition, beginners welcome

Performances in Estonia and Latvia, participation in festivals, and cultural events.
